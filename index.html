Web-Based Application Development

Fall 25

This course introduces students to the fundamental concepts and practices of web-based application development. Through a hands-on, project-driven approach, students will learn to design, develop, secure, and deploy full-stack applications. Emphasis is placed on progressive learning—beginning with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals, moving towards frontend frameworks, backend development, databases, security, and deployment. By the end of the semester, students will have built a complete, deployed application and gained practical experience with industry-standard tools and practices.
